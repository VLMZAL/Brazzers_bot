Substat Effects
Damage increases all weapon and skill damage.
Health increases base HP.
Ranged Damage affects only ranged weapons.
Melee Damage affects only melee weapons.
Skill Damage boosts skill power and buff effects.
Cooldown reduces skill activation time.
Regen restores HP every second.
Lifesteal converts weapon damage into HP.
Attack Speed increases hit frequency.
Double Chance gives a chance to strike twice.
Crit Chance and Crit Damage increase critical hit power.
Block reduces incoming hits.
Ranged weapons often outperform melee unless you have melee bonuses.
