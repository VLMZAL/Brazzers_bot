Substat Effects

Damage:Increases all weapon and skill damage
Health: Increases your base health by x %
Ranged Damage: Only affects ranged weapons
Melee Damage: Only affects melee weapons (useless without one equipped)
Skill Damage: Boosts skill power and buff skill HP and damage
Skill Cooldown: Reduces activation time of skills.
Health Regen: Restores HP every second, strong early on
Lifesteal: Converts a percentage of weapon damage into HP (pairs with Attack Speed and Double Chance)
Attack Speed: Faster attacks mean more hits and more lifesteal
Double Chance: Chance for main weapon to strike twice
Crit Chance: Chance to land a critical hit (base 120% damage)
Crit Damage: Raises crit multiplier, combine with Crit Chance
Block: Chance to block incoming hits
💡 Ranged weapons sometimes outperform melee unless you have specific bonuses or prefer melee playstyle.

⸻

Recommended Builds

Beginner Build (Early–Mid Game)
Priority order:
• Health Regen 20%+
• Double Chance 80%+
• Attack Speed 100%+
• Lifesteal 30%+

Higher Health Regen is very strong early on, especially for regular stages and dungeons, as it provides consistent survivability.
However, Regen becomes less effective in PvP, so try to balance it with the other stats for maximum efficiency. 
Full regen (40%+) builds can work however, but always keep an eye on your PvP performance. 

Once you reach Quantum gear (2 substats per item), Regen starts to fall off hard. At that point, combining Lifesteal with Crit becomes much stronger — a single crit hit can often heal half your HP or more.
